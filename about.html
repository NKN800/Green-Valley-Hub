Our Story

Green Valley Community Hub was born in March 2021, when three local entrepreneurs — Naomi Wambui, David Patel, and Lucas Mwangi — recognised a growing need for affordable workspace in the valley region. Remote work was on the rise, yet many residents lacked access to reliable internet, professional meeting rooms, or simply a place to focus away from home.

What began as a modest refurbishment of an old warehouse has blossomed into a thriving community of over 3,200 members. We've expanded from 12 desks to 85 workstations, added two soundproof meeting rooms, a podcast studio, and a beautiful event space that hosts everything from art exhibitions to coding bootcamps.

Our founding principle remains unchanged: everyone deserves access to a space where they can create, collaborate, and grow.

Our Mission

To cultivate an inclusive, sustainable community hub that empowers individuals through accessible workspace, lifelong learning opportunities, and meaningful human connection.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity — Everyone is welcome, regardless of background or budget.
  • Sustainability — We operate with eco-conscious practices, from solar panels to zero-waste policies.
  • Collaboration — We believe great things happen when people work together.
  • Growth — We support lifelong learning through workshops, mentoring, and resources.
